# Roadmap

## Completed

- Home, category, detail, cart, order, profile, login authorization, accessibility mode.
- Sign-in points and basic member interaction.
- Merchant backend foundation.
- Product management and order management.
- Partial statistics dashboard.
- AR preview concept and furniture placement interaction.
- AI Agent evidence page and online demo.

## Next Iterations

1. WeChat Pay merchant integration.
2. AR room scanning and anchor calibration.
3. GLB/USDZ model upload, compression, preview, and rotation.
4. AI customer service connected to product, order, invoice, and delivery knowledge.
5. User behavior analysis and dashboard export.
6. Merchant role permission, operation log, and audit flow.
7. Production mini-program submission and online validation.

## Release Goal

Deliver a usable WeChat mini-program plus merchant backend that lets users inspect furniture scale before buying and lets merchants manage products, models, orders, invoices, and sales data efficiently.
